Nintendhash#

题目描述#

Here’s a small hashing function, creating digests that are fictive video game names, called NintendHash. You will show that this function is not second pre-image resistant by finding a second pre-image.

Here’s the message : ”To catch them is my real test. To train them is my cause.”

You have to add a 64 bit nonce to that message, starting with value 0, and increment it until you find a second pre-image. The minimal such nonce is your flag (as an integer value), wrapped in the DOCTF{---} flag format.

You will find two python files attached: one containing the nintendhash function itself, and the other containing a basic example, the message and digest, and a recap of what you’re supposed to do.
