Crypto#

One Trick Pony#

题目描述#

I know a few secrets about this KAsino. But they would not be secrets if a shared them with everybody, so of course I do not share them in plaintext. Good luck getting past my information-theoretic secure encryption.This challenge is the first from a set of twothree challenges.

题目内容#

题目分析#

c 语言实现的一个异或加密，题目存在的漏洞点是 rand() 函数未播种，所以这个函数每次都生成同样的数字。

Crooked Roulette#

题目描述#

Your Seatmate is sure to know what you have to bet in the next Round of K-Roulette™. But he does not have the guts to bet it himself. Can you make the bet and win it all?

题目内容#

题目分析#

解出本题的关键是我们可以选择任意明文并获得其相应的签名，只需要注意到

$sig(m) = -1*sig(-m)$

所以可以发送 result 的相反数并送去签名，然后将得到的签名值变为其相反数就可以得到 result 的签名了，随后的步骤就是发送 result 和其签名获取 flag。
