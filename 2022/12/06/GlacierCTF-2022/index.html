    ChaCha60#

    题目描述#

    crypto

    Just decrypt the flag.

    Hint:

    The concrete values of the matrices are crucial for exploitation. They were not chosen at random

    解题步骤#

    本题是一个线性代数题，需要部分线代背景知识。

    m1,m2,m3是三个32行64列的矩阵，而key是一个64行1列的矩阵。所以k1、k2、k3为32行一列的矩阵（进行了模2运算，由0、1构成）。

    sagemath的矩阵相关函数和定义的文档地址：

    https://doc.sagemath.org/html/en/reference/matrices/sage/matrix/matrix2.html#sage.matrix.matrix2.Matrix.echelon_form

    1. those matrices 3 x 64 x 32 had not full rank if you combine them

    1. [20:22]

      so the 64bit key creates only 32 bits of entropy (已编辑)

    1. [20:22]

      so you calculate the basis matrix of the rank 32 reduced matrix (like 32 x 96) vectorspace

      1. then you enumerate all 2**32 linearcombinations of those basis vectors

      2. [20:23]

        for the 3 keys
